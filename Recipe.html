Recipe

Menu: Garlic Pepper Beef

  • 1 kg of Beef tapa
  • 1 Garlic
  • Small amount of Salt & Pepper
  • Oil or Butter
  • 1 can of Mushroom
  • Small amount of salt
  • Half a cup of milk
  • Half a cup of flour

Methods

  1. Step 1: In a pan put a small amount of oil or butter
  2. Step 2: Saute garlic
  3. Step 3: Put your 1 kg beef tapa in your pan and cook
  4. Step 4: Set aside your beef tapa in a plate
  5. Step 5: On the same pan put a small amount of oil or butter
  6. Step 6: Put a half cup of milk and flour
  7. Step 7: Wait your mixture to thicken
  8. Step 8: Put your cooked tapa in your pan
  9. Step 9: Put your salt and pepper
  10. Step 10: Your beef tapa is cooked
